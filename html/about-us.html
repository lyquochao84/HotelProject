Anaheim Carrage Inn

Our Story

Welcome to the Anaheim Carriage Inn and the ‘Happiest Place on Earth’, Disneyland! Our family-friendly hotel is situated just 2 blocks from Disneyland and Downtown Disney and just 1 block from the recently expanded Anaheim Convention Center. The immediate area offers a wide choice of shops and restaurants that appeals to all tastes.



Our Hotel offers the ideal ‘home base’ from which to explore this funifilled area. Start your day off enjoying our complimentary light breakfast and allow our multi-lingual staff to suggest exciting adventures during your visit. After a full day of activities, relax beside our outdoor heated pool and Jacuzzi tub.

The Anaheim Carriage Inn is completely ADA compliant, from the registration desk to our handicap-accessible rooms on the ground floor. These rooms are specially equipped with everything from a walk in shower with appropriate bathroom safety bars to a TTY machine for use by the hearing impaired.



We offer free parking as well as free WiFi and laundry facilities. Our staff is available 24 hours a day to assist with any needs. Floors 2 and 3 are easily accessible by elevator.
